Objective

Collect the most stars before both players die by falling off the bottom of the stage! The bars on each side of the screen represent their respective player's scores.

Controls

R: Restart

Destroy clusters of blocks by running into them.

Player 1

W: Jump
A: Left
S: Down
D: Right

Player 2

Up: Jump
Left: Left
Down: Down
Right: Right
