Objective

  • Destroy clusters of blocks by running into them.
  • Jewels and ores power up your tools.
  • Try to knock your opponent off the bottom of the screen!

Controls

R: Restart
M: Mute/Unmute

Player 1

W: Jump
A: Left
S: Down
D: Right
C: Aim/Fire

Player 2

Up: Jump
Left: Left
Down: Down
Right: Right
Space: Aim/Fire
